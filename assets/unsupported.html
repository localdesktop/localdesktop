Device Unsupported!

Local Desktop runs on top of PRoot. Unfortunately, your device's Android kernel is missing the required system calls to run PRoot correctly.

What you can try:

  • Move Local Desktop out of a special file system, such as an SD card.
  • Disable any special conditions or modifications if your device is rooted.
  • Use Termux & proot-distro on this device.
  • Use another device.
  • Leave a comment on this GitHub issue with the details of your device, including: device model, Android version, special conditions (e.g. rooted, custom ROM, SD card, etc.)

And please star our project to show your interest and keep us motivated to support more devices: github.com/localdesktop/localdesktop
